client

BAPCO
About project

This multimedia multi-genre show was created to launch a new logo for Bahrain's oil and gas company, Bapco Energies, one of the largest industry companies in the region. It was not only the launch of the logo, but also the presentation of the vision for the development of Bahrain until 2040.

About project

We use a lot of expressive means for this show: Robo hands with led screens for which unique content with dynamic mapping was created, screens using the effect of anamorphic illusions, large-scale mapping on the buildings of the National Museum of Bahrain and a drone show, for the first time in the world creating an interactive between projection mapping and drone show.

We are proud that this grandiose project was implemented in just 3 weeks of preparation, but became a landmark for all Bahrainis.
